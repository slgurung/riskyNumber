RiskyNumber:

RiskyNumber is a web platform for financial information analytics, primarily for the stock market data. This is not a commercial product. This platform is being developed for personal education & research purposes only. At the moment, this platform is in it's very early stages of development.

The majority of information is derived from the various websites using numerous web-scraping technologies based on Python. I can't be sure about the accuracy of financial data and other information you find in this platform. In the future, the financial data and information might be imported to this app from reliable commercial sources if it is feasible. Until then, this is just an ongoing personal research project. I hope this app will become a great source for the investors in the very near future. Please visit the platform more frequently for the updates. Thank you for your visit today.
